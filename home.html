What is Google AdSense?

  • A program that lets you earn money by displaying ads on your website or YouTube channel.
  • Works on CPC (Cost Per Click) and CPM (Cost Per 1,000 Impressions).
  • Easy integration with websites and blogs.

Benefits

  • High-quality, relevant ads from Google's network.
  • Real-time performance tracking and analytics.

What is Google Analytics?

Digital marketing refers to the use of digital channels like search engines, social media, email, and websites to promote products or services. It's important because it allows businesses to reach a global audience, track campaign performance in real-time, and target specific demographics for better ROI.

Anyone interested in marketing, business, or technology can take a digital marketing course. It's ideal for students, job seekers, entrepreneurs, marketing professionals, and even small business owners who want to enhance their online presence.

Most courses cover essential topics such as Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Search Engine Marketing (SEM), Social Media Marketing, Email Marketing, Content Marketing, Google Ads, Google Analytics, and Affiliate Marketing.

Generally, no technical background is required. Basic computer skills and an interest in online marketing are usually enough. However, familiarity with the internet and social media platforms can be beneficial.
